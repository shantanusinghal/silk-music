Customer Reviews

Disappointed
     
by Asher Miller

I'm a huge Cascada fan but this is just a disappoinment. I like that there exploring with diffrent types of dance music but "Madness" doesn't have that Cascada feel to it (Atleast with "Original Me" it had a Cascada vibe to it even though it was a Pop Album). I love Natalie's part, her voice is great as always but the feat. rapper Tris's part gets annoying after a while and he sounds to much like Pitbull. I don't like how Natalie only sings the chorus. The song feels like "Tris feat. Cascada". The beat is ok but it's genertic. The song ends aburptly and the ending is also messy. In my opinion I don't think Base Drop styled dance music works for Cascada. I hope with the next release or Album they stray away from this style. In all I look forward to furture releases by them.

Cascada slays again!
     
by persingern

In my four years of listening to every Cascada song there is, this may be the first one that took me by surprise. It's atypical, which prior to their last album has gotten them big hits like Everytime We Touch and Evacuate the Dancefloor, both of which shocked people. Cascada may have just cracked their slump. Time will tell, but my feeling is: this song is the best song of 2014. x <3

Biography

Formed: 2004 in Bonn, Germany

Genre: Dance

Years Active: '00s, '10s

Originally called Cascade until legal conflicts forced them to change their name, Cascada is the slick, uplifting Euro-dance trio featuring producers DJ Manian and Yanou along with vocalist Natalie Horler, although Horler is sometimes presented as Cascada herself. Horler was born in Bonn, Germany, to English parents and grew up loving all sorts of music.
