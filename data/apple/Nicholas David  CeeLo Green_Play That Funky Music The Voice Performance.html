Biography

Born: St. Paul, MN

Genre: Pop

Years Active: '10s

Soulful vocalist Nicholas David finished in third place on the third season of the NBC television show The Voice. Born Nicholas David Mrozinski in St. Paul, Minnesota, David grew up studying piano and singing and listening to classical and jazz music before delving into pop songwriting as an adult. He released several independent solo albums under the name Nicholas Mrozinski, including 2008's Oak Chase Way, 2009's Together We're Stronger, and 2011's In Formation.
