iTunes Review

Speculation about the musical portion of the Twilight franchise is beginning to rival the potential plot scuttlebutt about what happens next to Edward and Bella. For the soundtrack to Eclipse, the mystery is gone. Music by Muse, Metric, Beck with Bat For Lashes, Vampire Weekend, the Bravery, the Black Keys, the Dead Weather, Cee-Lo Green, Sia, Fanfario, Florence & The Machine, UNKLE, Band of Horses, and Eastern Conference Champions.

Best one yet!!
     
by hp07

This is by far the best soundtrack yet! I really loved twilight for the more rock/alternative music, it was great music that anyone could love. New Moon was a little bit more indie but all the artist selections were great and fit into the film beautifully. Eclipse on the otherhand is a happy mix of both rock/alternative and indie. My personal favorites are Eclipse (All Yours), Heavy In Your Arms, Ours, and Chop and Change.

This was is a great selection of songs and I cannot wait to see how they will fit into the film!
