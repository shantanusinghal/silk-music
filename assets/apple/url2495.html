A great find for any M5 fan
     
by tralalaman

I have loved and followed this band for a couple years now, and by chance I came across this album tonight. Unfortunately, I don't have enough money in my account to purchase it right now, at least not the entire album, so take this review with a grain of salt. That being said, I have some slightly mixed feelings. I've never been a big fan of "Makes Me Wonder" or "Wake Up Call" when they're live, I just don't think that they pack the power they do on the studio album. They are still great songs here, they just aren't the same as their original releases. Any fault is also made up for by "Won't Go Home Without You" and "Nothing Lasts Forever," both of which I would highly recommend for download. I have not heard "If I Never See Your Face Again" yet in a live version, but it is surprisingly is the most promising of all the tracks, at least to me. I have not yet heard all of "Little of Your Time," and am not really sure so far what to make of it. I'm not normally a fan of small setting concerts. I'll take stadium rock any day of the week over "intimate" performances, and yet this recording pulls off a smaller setting very well. This is definitely a great, if somewhat short, sampling of their live work. I highly recommend it and will certainly be downloading most if not all of it myself.
