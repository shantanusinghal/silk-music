Biography

Born: March 23, 1989 in Marietta, GA

Genre: Hip-Hop/Rap

Years Active: '00s, '10s

Mixing hip-hop, R&B, and pop, producer and recording artist Mike WiLL Made It had hits in all those genres, ranging from the street-level club track "Bandz a Make Her Dance" to the Top Ten pop hit "We Can't Stop." Born Michael Len Williams II in Marietta, Georgia, WiLL enrolled at Georgia State but dropped out to focus on music production. His first big gig happened when he sold beats to rapper Gucci Mane.
